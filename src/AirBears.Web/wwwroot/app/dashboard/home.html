Air Bears

Volunteer Search & Rescue

Specialized in creating & maintaining a global network of volunteer RC pilots who are dedicated to assisting local authorities in the search & rescue of missing persons, or persons of interest.

Air Bears works along side local sheriff and fire departments to help them in any way we can. Giving the command personnel a birds eye view of the operation on site, in real time is very valuable for safety.

When authorities ask for help with a search, the plan goes into action. Those who are available to help will contact and coordinate with the search organizer, arrive at the location and begin search operations.

Emergencies happen all the time and anytime. With Air Bear volunteer pilots around the globe, our assistance is invaluable to many people involved with many situations, saving time, resources and possibly lives.
